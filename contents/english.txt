```Special Rule 4 — Dental Purification:```

Performing *Thandhasuththi*
with one of these (herbals) —
* Potralaikkaiyaanthakarai 
(Eclipta Prostrata),  
* Karisalaangkanni 
(Wedelia Calandulacea)

— as a little essence goes inside and
kapha(phlegm)-water, pitta(bile)-water come outside.

– *Thiru Arutprakasa Vallalar*
```(Nityā Kārma Vidhi – Sirappu Vidhi)```
